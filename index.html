Ivan Pecorari

Experience Designer

A designer at the core, I am passionate and curious about how the physical can meet the digital and how the impact of new interactive technologies will deliver innovative solutions for the global and local challenges of tomorrow’s world. Over the last 10 years, I have conceptualized and produced playful and meaningful interactive experiences. They ranged from mobile applications for disadvantaged communities in South Africa, an 18 meter-long interactive wall in Miami to a web tool for the Greater London Authority, that helps to form better decisions for future infrastructures plans of the City. I recently led Videogames: Design/Play/Disrupt, a major exhibition at V&A.
